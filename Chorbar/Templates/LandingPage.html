Welcome to Chor.bar

Chor.bar helps track chores, and match expectations with the rest of the household.

Agree on how often things should get done, tick them off as you go, and get a clear shared picture of what your household actually needs. No nagging required.

  • Feel unseen vacuuming for the 20th time?
  • Wonder if anyone else has noticed the bathroom hasn't been cleaned in weeks?
  • Disagree about what "clean enough" actually means?
  • Or just want to build a new habit that sticks?
Get to the Chor.bar →

Chor.bar is young and growing, built from the real needs of our own household — where we're de-Googling and trying to use technology a little more mindfully. Coming soon: shopping lists, meal plans, reminders, integrations and much more!
