Why?

Chor.bar is young and growing, built from the real needs of our own household — where we're de-Googling and trying to use technology a little more mindfully.

Features:

  1. Track chores
  2. Invite family members
  3. Set goals (how often should they be done?
  4. Integrate with your calendar (via .ical)
  5. Shopping lists, with categories and much more! new
